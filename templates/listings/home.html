About Rental Connect

Rental Connect is a role-based rental management system designed to simplify the interaction between tenants and property owners.

Unlike traditional listing platforms, Rental Connect focuses on verified properties, controlled booking workflows, and availability validation to reduce conflicts and improve trust.

The system applies algorithmic checks to prevent overlapping bookings and enforces role-specific access, ensuring that tenants, owners, and administrators interact only with relevant features.
